# The Starlight Covenant
*"By ancient wisdom we guard, by tested steel we protect"*

## Overview
The Starlight Covenant, commonly known as the Adventurers' Guild, is an ancient organization that traces its roots back to the original orders of monster hunters and curse breakers. While many see them simply as adventurers for hire, the Covenant maintains a deeper purpose: protecting the realms from supernatural threats using both ancient knowledge and martial prowess.

## Structure

### Leadership
- **The Astral Council**: Seven veteran members who guide the organization
- **Regional Commanders**: Experienced members like Lyra Steelfist who oversee operations in specific territories
- **Guild Assessors**: Specialists like Theron Blackwood who evaluate both threats and members
- **Lore Keepers**: Scholars who maintain the Guild's vast knowledge of supernatural threats

### Ranks
1. **Initiate**: New members under evaluation
2. **Covenant Hunter**: Full members cleared for regular missions
3. **Veteran**: Experienced members with proven track records
4. **Master**: Elite members who specialize in specific types of threats
5. **Commander**: Leaders who coordinate large-scale operations
6. **Assessor**: Specialists who evaluate threats and members
7. **Council Member**: The highest rank, reserved for the most accomplished members

## Headquarters
The main headquarters is located in an ancient fortress called Starlight Keep, built on the ruins of an old observatory. Regional chapters are established in major cities, with Silvercrest hosting one of the largest branches.

## Core Principles
1. **Knowledge Before Action**: Understanding a threat is the first step to defeating it
2. **Protect the Innocent**: The safety of civilians is paramount
3. **Honor the Ancient Ways**: Respect and learn from historical methods of monster hunting
4. **Strength in Unity**: No member fights alone
5. **Learn and Adapt**: Each encounter is an opportunity to improve

## Notable Achievements
- Breaking the Curse of the Crimson Moon
- Ending the Siege of Frostspire
- Containing the Plague of Shadows
- Mapping the major ley lines of the Northern Kingdoms
- Maintaining the most comprehensive library of supernatural threats and their remedies

## Current Challenges
- The rising frequency of supernatural incidents
- The emergence of new types of curses
- The loss of ancient knowledge
- The need for more qualified members
- The political pressure from various kingdoms

## Recruitment
The Covenant recruits from various backgrounds:
- Military veterans
- Academic scholars
- Experienced monster hunters
- Gifted magic users
- Those who have survived supernatural encounters

## Training
New members undergo rigorous training in:
- Combat against supernatural entities
- Recognition of cursed objects and locations
- Basic curse breaking techniques
- Survival in hostile environments
- Team coordination and communication

## Equipment
The Covenant maintains its own:
- Armory of enchanted weapons
- Library of curse-breaking texts
- Collection of protective artifacts
- Network of safe houses
- Supply of specialized monster-hunting tools

## Notes

Our methods may seem unconventional to some, but they are tested by time and blood."* - From the journals of the First Council
