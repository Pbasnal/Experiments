# The People's Covenant
*"When hope seems lost, look for the silver badge"*

## Origins
The People's Covenant, commonly known as the Adventurers' Guild, was founded in Silvercrest during the early years of the self-appointed ruler's reign. As the city's official forces became increasingly focused on maintaining the ruler's power and control, many citizens - especially those of non-human races - found themselves without protection or recourse for help.

The Guild was formed by a diverse group of former soldiers, magic users, and skilled individuals who saw the growing disparity in how different races were treated within Silvercrest. They created an organization that would serve all people, regardless of their race, status, or political connections.

## Core Principles
1. **Equal Protection for All**: Aid is given based on need, not race or status
2. **Independence from Crown**: No political allegiance to maintain objectivity
3. **Fair Payment System**: Sliding scale fees based on client's means
4. **Open Membership**: Anyone with skills and good intent can join
5. **Community First**: Priority given to protecting civilian populations

## Structure

### Leadership
- **The Council of Equals**: Representatives from different races and backgrounds
- **Regional Leaders**: Experienced members who coordinate local operations
- **Guild Assessors**: Evaluate both missions and member capabilities
- **Community Liaisons**: Maintain relationships with different racial communities

### Membership Requirements
- Demonstrated skill in relevant fields
- Commitment to protecting all races equally
- Clean record of treating all races fairly
- Willingness to work with diverse teams
- Acceptance of the Guild's independent stance

## Operations

### Services Offered
- Protection for marginalized communities
- Investigation of crimes ignored by official forces
- Escort services for threatened individuals
- Monster and threat elimination
- Mediation between different racial groups

### Funding
- Mission fees (scaled to client ability)
- Donations from wealthy sympathizers
- Guild-owned businesses
- Training services
- Equipment sales and rentals

## Relationship with Authority

### With the Crown
- Maintain professional distance
- Avoid political entanglement
- Operate within law while preserving independence
- Diplomatic but firm stance on autonomy

### With City Guard
- Cooperative when possible
- Non-confrontational but independent
- Fill protection gaps without undermining authority
- Share information about major threats

## Notable Achievements
- Establishing safe routes for non-human merchants
- Creating mixed-race patrol teams
- Building trust across racial communities
- Developing fair employment practices
- Maintaining independence despite political pressure

## Facilities
- Main Guild Hall (open to all races)
- Training grounds
- Safe houses throughout the city
- Community meeting spaces
- Equipment workshops

## Current Challenges
- Balancing independence with legal compliance
- Managing growth while maintaining principles
- Protecting members from political pressure
- Securing funding without compromising values
- Maintaining unity among diverse membership

## Cultural Impact
The Guild has become a symbol of hope in Silvercrest, particularly among non-human races. Their silver badge is recognized as a promise of fair treatment and protection. They've created a model of how different races can work together effectively, challenging the city's institutional biases.

## Future Goals
- Expand services to outlying areas
- Develop more training programs
- Strengthen inter-racial cooperation
- Build more community centers
- Establish legal advocacy services

## Notes
*"We formed this guild not just to fight monsters or earn coin, but to show that in a city divided by race and status, there can be a place where everyone stands equal. Our badge means the same thing to everyone - help is here, and it's here for all."* - From the Guild's founding charter
