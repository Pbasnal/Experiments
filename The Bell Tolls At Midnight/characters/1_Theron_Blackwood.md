# Theron Blackwood - The Invigilator
*Former Knight of Silvercrest, Guild Assessor*

## Physical Description
- Age: Late 40s
- Appearance: Tall and weathered, with silver-streaked black hair and a neatly trimmed beard
- Notable Features: 
  - A collection of small silver rings in his left ear, each representing a successful mission
  - Deep scar running from his right temple to jaw
  - Carries himself with the unmistakable bearing of formal military training
  - Wears a simple silver pendant of Silvercrest's crown beneath his clothes

## Background
Born and raised in Silvercrest, the jewel of the Northern Kingdoms, Theron served as a distinguished knight in the city's regular forces. His life changed when he witnessed a young commander-in-training named Lyra Steelfist demonstrating exceptional combat prowess in the training yards. Her dedication, skill, and the hint of sadness in her eyes sparked something in him - a desire to follow a similar path of protecting others from supernatural threats.

### The Path Not Taken
While Lyra quickly rose through the ranks to become commander of the Crown's Aegis (Silvercrest's elite monster-hunting unit that reports directly to the king), Theron worked tirelessly to improve his skills, hoping to one day join the unit himself. However, before he could achieve this goal, Lyra left her position to become a commander in the Adventurers' Guild.

Without hesitation, Theron resigned from his prestigious position as a knight and joined the Guild, following in her footsteps. His decision puzzled many - why would a respected knight of Silvercrest abandon his post for the uncertain life of an adventurer? The truth of his inspiration remains his closest-kept secret.

### Military Experience
- Former Knight of Silvercrest's City Guard
- Trained under Master-at-Arms Viktor Dawnforge
- Specialized in defensive combat and protection tactics
- Earned multiple commendations for protecting civilians during supernatural incursions

## Connection to Silvercrest

### The City of Silvercrest
- Capital of the Northern Territories
- Known for its towering silver spires and ancient magical defenses
- Home to the prestigious Knight's Academy
- Protected by both conventional military and specialized monster-hunting units

### The Crown's Aegis
- Elite monster-hunting unit reporting directly to the King of Silvercrest
- Comprised of hand-picked soldiers with exceptional combat abilities
- Specializes in supernatural threat elimination
- Known for their distinctive silver-runed armor and enchanted weapons
- Previously commanded by Lyra Steelfist before she joined the Guild

## Current Role
As a Guild Assessor, Theron has found a way to honor both his knightly training and his admiration for Lyra's approach to protecting others. His experience in both formal military structure and supernatural combat makes him uniquely qualified to evaluate both threats and those who would face them.

## Personal Code
- Maintains the formal discipline of a Silvercrest knight
- Never speaks of his true reasons for joining the Guild
- Channels his unrequited feelings into protecting others
- Strives to embody the ideals he admired in Lyra's leadership

## Notes
*"A knight's duty is to protect - whether that's behind city walls or in cursed towns like this. The path doesn't matter as much as the purpose."*
