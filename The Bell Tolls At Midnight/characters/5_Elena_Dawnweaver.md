# Elena Dawnweaver - The Healer
*Spirit Medium and Restoration Specialist*

## Physical Description
- Age: Early 30s
- Appearance: Average height, with long silver-blonde hair often braided with healing herbs
- Notable Features:
  - Mismatched eyes (one amber, one pale blue)
  - Intricate tattoos of healing runes across her forearms
  - Carries a staff wrapped with flowering vines that never wither
  - Wears practical robes with numerous pouches for herbs and medical supplies

## Background
Elena comes from a long line of healers who specialized in treating supernatural ailments. Her unique ability to communicate with spirits makes her both a valuable healer and a dangerous target for malevolent entities. She joined the Guild after realizing that many curses required healing not just bodies, but places and spirits as well.

### Unique Abilities
- Can sense and communicate with spirits
- Able to heal both physical and spiritual wounds
- Can detect cursed influences on living beings
- Skilled in protective and purifying rituals
- Understanding of the connection between physical and spiritual health

## Personality
- Compassionate but practical
- Calm under pressure
- Sometimes troubled by the spirits she senses
- Determined to help both the living and the dead
- Carries the weight of those she couldn't save

## Medical Expertise
- Traditional healing methods
- Supernatural wound treatment
- Curse-related ailment specialist
- Expert in herbal remedies
- Understanding of spiritual afflictions

## Equipment
- Staff of Healing and Spirit Communication
- Extensive collection of medicinal herbs
- Holy symbols from various faiths
- Ritual components for purification
- Emergency medical supplies

## Strengths
- Unique perspective on supernatural problems
- Strong healing abilities
- Ability to communicate with spirits
- Extensive knowledge of curses' effects on living beings
- Skilled in protective rituals

## Weaknesses
- Vulnerable to spiritual possession
- Can be overwhelmed by too many spiritual presences
- Sometimes too empathetic for her own good
- Difficulty ignoring calls for help
- Struggles with spirits she can't help

## Notes
*"Every curse leaves a wound—not just on places and people, but on the very fabric of reality. Sometimes, breaking a curse isn't enough. You need to heal what it broke, or it'll just come back in a different form. The tower... it's like an infected wound in the world itself."*

## Current Focus
- Understanding the curse's effect on townspeople
- Studying the pattern of spiritual activity
- Preparing healing rituals for various scenarios
- Developing protection methods for the team
- Investigating possible spiritual anchors in the tower

## Concerns about Gravedawn
- The deep spiritual wounds in the town
- The connection between the tower and the townspeople's health
- The possibility of mass spiritual trauma
- The winter solstice's potential effect on healing magic
- The risk of spiritual contamination during healing attempts
