# Lyra Steelfist - The Warrior
*Former Military Commander turned Monster Hunter*

## Physical Description
- Age: Early 30s
- Appearance: Muscular build, short auburn hair with a white streak
- Notable Features:
  - Intricate armor with reinforced joints for mobility
  - Collection of various weapon types strapped across her back and belt
  - Distinctive gauntlets with magical enhancements for combat
  - Burns on her right arm from a previous encounter with supernatural forces

## Background
Lyra served as a commander in the Northern Kingdoms' elite monster-hunting unit before joining the Adventurers' Guild. Her experience comes from actual combat rather than academic study, making her approach more practical than theoretical.

### Military Experience
- Led successful campaigns against supernatural threats in the Northern Territories
- Trained special units in combat against non-conventional enemies
- Developed new tactics for fighting incorporeal threats
- Survived the Siege of Frostspire where her unit faced waves of possessed soldiers

## Personality
- Direct and straightforward in communication
- Natural leader but willing to follow when necessary
- Protective of civilians and team members
- Struggles with patience in strategic situations
- Has a strong code of honor that sometimes conflicts with pragmatic solutions

## Combat Expertise
- Master of multiple weapon types
- Specialized in fighting supernatural and incorporeal enemies
- Expert in tactical positioning and group combat coordination
- Developed techniques for fighting in cursed environments

## Equipment
- Enchanted bastard sword with ghost-touch properties
- Reinforced armor with protective runes
- Various specialized weapons for different types of supernatural threats
- Magical gauntlets that can channel protective energy

## Strengths
- Exceptional combat skills
- Experience fighting supernatural threats
- Strong tactical mind
- Natural leadership abilities
- Adaptable fighting style

## Weaknesses
- Can be too aggressive in approach
- Sometimes overlooks subtle magical threats
- Tendency to prioritize direct action over investigation
- Past trauma from losing squad members affects decision-making

## Notes
*"I've seen what happens when you underestimate these things. They're not just monsters you can beat down—they're clever, patient, and they know how to use your own strength against you. But that doesn't mean they can't be fought. Everything can be fought if you know how."*

## Current Mission Concerns
- Worried about the number of civilian casualties already suffered
- Interested in understanding why previous military approaches failed
- Concerned about the team's ability to coordinate in supernatural conditions
- Wants to establish clear combat protocols before entering the tower
