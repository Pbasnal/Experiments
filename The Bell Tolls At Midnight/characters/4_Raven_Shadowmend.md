# Raven Shadowmend - The Scout
*Ghost Hunter and Information Specialist*

## Physical Description
- Age: Late 20s
- Appearance: Lithe and graceful, with dark skin and black hair kept in tight braids
- Notable Features:
  - Eyes that seem to catch light like a cat's
  - Multiple small scars on her hands from lockpicking
  - Moves with an unnaturally quiet grace
  - Wears flexible leather armor with numerous hidden compartments

## Background
Raven grew up in the shadow of another cursed location, learning from childhood how to survive in supernaturally dangerous environments. She developed her skills as an investigator and scout, specializing in gathering information about supernatural threats without alerting them to her presence.

### Experience
- Trained by the legendary ghost hunter Marina Dusk
- Survived three years solo investigating haunted locations
- Developed new techniques for tracking supernatural entities
- Expert in urban exploration and information gathering

## Personality
- Quiet and observant
- Prefers gathering information before acting
- Deeply pragmatic approach to supernatural threats
- Dry sense of humor
- Protective of civilian populations

## Specialized Skills
- Expert in stealth and surveillance
- Skilled investigator
- Proficient in reading supernatural signs and omens
- Advanced knowledge of urban exploration
- Experienced in escape artistry

## Equipment
- Enchanted daggers that can harm spiritual entities
- Collection of specialized tools for investigation
- Magical devices for detecting supernatural presence
- Grappling hook and climbing gear
- Various alchemical substances for dealing with different types of entities

## Strengths
- Exceptional stealth abilities
- Strong investigative skills
- Experience with supernatural entities
- Practical approach to problems
- Ability to work independently

## Weaknesses
- Prefers working alone
- Can be overly cautious
- Trust issues from past experiences
- Sometimes misses obvious solutions while looking for hidden ones
- Difficulty sharing information until fully verified

## Notes
*"The thing about cursed places is that they have patterns—like fingerprints. If you know how to read them, they'll tell you their stories. This tower... its pattern is strange. It's like it's writing its story in a language I've never seen before."*

## Current Investigation
- Mapping safe routes through Gravedawn
- Investigating patterns in disappearances
- Studying the tower's influence on the town
- Gathering local stories and testimonies
- Identifying potential safe zones and danger areas

## Concerns
- Unusual pattern of supernatural activity
- Lack of survivors from previous expeditions
- Complex relationship between town and tower
- Potential hidden threats in seemingly safe areas
