# The New Recruits

## Lyra Blackthorn
**Appearance**: Half-elf, 24, tall and lean with long black hair usually tied in intricate braids. Wears expensive but practical traveling clothes, often adorned with subtle silver threading.
**Background**: Noble daughter of a wealthy merchant
**Reason for Adventuring**: Suffocating under her father's controlling nature and arranged marriage plans. Trained in secret with traveling sword masters, using her "embroidery money" to pay for lessons. Sees adventuring as both escape and a way to prove she's more than just a marriage prospect.

Growing up in luxury didn't stop Lyra from feeling like a prisoner in her own home. Her elven mother died when she was young, and her human father became overprotective to the point of suffocation. While he was busy arranging political marriages, Lyra was secretly learning swordplay from retired adventurers, paying them with jewelry and allowance money. The final straw came when her father announced her engagement to a man thrice her age. She gathered her savings, took her mother's sword, and left a note saying she'd return when she had made her own name in the world.

## Korg "Gentle" Stonefist
**Appearance**: Half-orc, 32, massive frame but with surprisingly soft features. Bald with ritual scarification patterns on his scalp. Wears simple clothes with a carefully maintained leather apron.
**Background**: Former blacksmith and reluctant pit fighter
**Reason for Adventuring**: Needs money for his daughter's medicine, which is rare and expensive. Regular work doesn't pay enough, and he's trying to leave his pit fighting days behind.

Korg was content with his life as a blacksmith until his daughter fell ill with a rare magical ailment. The local healers can treat her, but the medicine costs more than he can make at the forge. He returned to pit fighting, where he earned his ironic nickname "Gentle" for apologizing to his opponents while defeating them, but the guilt of violence weighs heavy on him. The Adventurers' Guild offers a chance at honest work that pays enough for the medicine.

## Sasha "Swift" Redwood
**Appearance**: Human, 28, short and wiry with wild red hair. Multiple small scars on her hands. Moves with quick, nervous energy. Wears dark, practical clothes with many hidden pockets.
**Background**: Former thief and occasional smuggler
**Reason for Adventuring**: Wrongly accused of a major heist, needs to build a legitimate reputation to clear her name. Also needs money to support her younger siblings after their parents' death.

Sasha was the best thief in her small crew until someone framed her for stealing from the royal treasury. While she's done her share of theft, she never touched that job. Now no crew will work with her, fearing royal attention. With three younger siblings to feed and a reputation to rebuild, the Guild offers a chance at legitimacy. She's determined to find out who framed her, but first, she needs to establish herself as a trustworthy adventurer.

## Malik Al-Rashid
**Appearance**: Human, 35, dark-skinned with a neatly trimmed beard and keen eyes. Carries himself with scholarly dignity. Wears colorful robes from his homeland with practical modifications for traveling.
**Background**: Disgraced scholar and former merchant
**Reason for Adventuring**: Lost his family's fortune in a bad trade deal, needs to rebuild wealth to restore his family's honor and his position in the academy.

Once a respected scholar and successful merchant from distant lands, Malik's pursuit of ancient artifacts led him to invest his family's wealth in what turned out to be elaborate fakes. The scandal cost him his academic position and his family's savings. Rather than face shame at home, he's turned to adventuring to rebuild his fortune and hopefully discover genuine artifacts to restore his academic reputation.

## Thistle
**Appearance**: Halfling, 26, small even for her race with autumn-brown skin and moss-green hair. Always barefoot, wears clothes that seem to be made of leaves and bark but are actually cleverly dyed cloth.
**Background**: Former farmer/herbalist
**Reason for Adventuring**: Her village was destroyed by bandits while she was away gathering herbs. Seeks the strength and skills to protect others from similar fates.

Thistle (she never shares her full name) was her village's herbalist, learning from her grandmother. During a long herb-gathering trip, bandits razed her village, killing most of the inhabitants. She arrived too late to do anything but treat the few survivors. The guilt of not being there drives her to become stronger, while her knowledge of herbs and healing gives her a practical skill for adventuring groups.

## The Group Dynamic
They all arrived at the Guild hall on the same rainy morning - Lyra with her fine clothes soaked through, Korg ducking to fit through the door, Sasha casing the room out of habit, Malik trying to keep his papers dry, and Thistle leaving muddy footprints despite the caretaker's protests. With all other potential recruits delayed by the storm, they were grouped together for their initial assessment. Despite their differences (or perhaps because of them), they worked surprisingly well together, each bringing unique skills and perspectives to their test missions.
