## üéØ Image Generation Guidelines

### **General Requirements:**
- **Quality**: High resolution, professional quality
- **Style**: Modern, clean, professional
- **Colors**: Vibrant but not overwhelming
- **Accessibility**: Ensure good contrast for text overlays
- **Cultural Sensitivity**: Respectful representation of Indian culture
- **No Text**: All images should be text-free for flexibility

### **Technical Specifications:**
- **Format**: JPG for backgrounds, PNG for illustrations (with transparency)
- **Compression**: Optimized for web (file size under 500KB for backgrounds, under 200KB for illustrations)
- **Aspect Ratios**: Maintain specified ratios for proper display
- **Responsive**: Images should look good on different screen sizes

### **Theme Compatibility:**
- **Light Theme**: Images should work well with light text overlays
- **Dark Theme**: Consider how images will appear with dark mode
- **Contrast**: Ensure sufficient contrast for text readability
